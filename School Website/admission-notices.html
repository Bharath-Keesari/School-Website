Admission

ADMISSION POLICY & PROCEDURE

Application forms are issued by the school for upcoming academic year 2020-21 for classes LKG to Seventh. Timings: 10-00 a.m. and 05-00 p.m. on all weekdays Completed application forms should be submitted with all the enclosures and prescribed registration fee on or before the date specified by the School. The Registration fee once paid will not be refunded or adjusted for any other purpose. Mere Registration is no guarantee for admissions. Age for Admissions: LKG : 3 years and above UKG : 4 years and above Class I : 5 years and above The age is reckoned as on 31st March of the year of admission.

Documents to be submitted:

• Completed Application Form • Passport size photos of the student and both the Parents should be pasted in the form. • Copy of Birth Certificate from Panchayat/ Municipal Corporation (Bring original certificate for verification and it will be returned). • Certificate of caste / Physically Challenged (SC/BC/ST/PC) (if applicable). • Copy of residence proof of present address (like Telephone bill, Electricity Bill, Passport). • Copy of Aadhar Card (Child, Father, Mother).

ADMISSION POLICY & PROCEDURE

Oral interviews are conducted for LKG, UKG and 1st kids. From 2nd onwards, kids need to go through a written test and result of which will be announced and communicated to parents.
